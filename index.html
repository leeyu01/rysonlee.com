Research Experience

During Summer 2025, I participated in the High School Research Program (HSRP) at Brookhaven National Laboratory.

My research project focused on the FPGA-Based PI Controller and EPICS Control System for the NSLS-II Electrometer . Through this project, I collaborated with scientists and engineers to explore control systems, embedded hardware, and instrumentation used at the National Synchrotron Light Source II.

This experience strengthened my interests in electrical engineering, computer engineering, FPGA development, EPICS-based control systems, software development, and large-scale scientific research.

Engineering & Innovation

Be My Access

I founded Be My Access , an AI-powered accessibility project designed to support people who are blind or have low vision.

The project uses artificial intelligence to collect, organize, summarize, and read important information aloud for users. By helping users access websites, documents, and online resources more independently, Be My Access aims to make digital information easier to understand and more accessible.

Through this project, I combine software engineering, artificial intelligence, and community service to create practical tools that can improve everyday life for people with vision disabilities.
